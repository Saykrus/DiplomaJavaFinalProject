Creating new Accounts:

You can create an account by filling in the details in the left hand fields.
'Label' - to determine what the account is for.
'Username' - the username for the desired account.
'Password' - the password for the desired account.
'Email' - the email address for the desired account
'Pin' - a field you can save the 'pin' for the desired account if it has one.
NOTE: All fields except 'pin' are mandatory to create an account.


After you click create, the account will be created and stored in the table on the right.
